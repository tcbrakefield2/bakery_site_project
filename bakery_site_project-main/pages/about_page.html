About Us

La Belle Bakery is a baked goods company that specializes in sourdough, cookies, and macarons. We believe in “the beauty of handmade” adding love, passion, and creativity in all we create. We strive for a world of food and sweets that you can know everything it contains and we are very open about our process and ingredients.
